01. About

George Greaves

George Greaves is a Bristol based Designer. His work is bold and graphic with an eye for the unusual. He has had his work featured in It's Nice That, Architectural Digest, Crack, Esquire, Time Out. He has worked with clients such as Apple, Hennessy, Rimowa, Apartmento Studios, Soho House, Bulgari, The Financial Times, Stella Artios, HSBC, Peroni, Bandcamp, Süddeutsche Zeitung and La Parisienne.

02. Printed Goods

Printed Goods

We aim to deliver within 2-3 working days (Mon-Fri) for UK orders- shipped with Royal Mail, 4-10 working days for EU orders and 10-18 working days for Rest of the World orders.

How soon will I receive my order?

On rare occasions parcels may go missing, if for any reason you have not received your parcel within 7 working days of ordering (UK), please email us at hello@printedgoods.net and allow us 5-8 working days to carry out the investigation.

Delivery Exceptions

Returns - Exchanges

We guarantee to refund any full price item you are not completely happy with when you return it to us within 14 days of receipt.

Returns by Post

If you return goods via post please note that we are not liable for the costs. Please note that we are not liable for items lost in the post. Returned goods must be packaged securely as we will not accept returned goods that are damaged in transit.

Statutory Rights

Your statutory rights are not affected by our return policy.

Duties

We recommend contacting your local customs office for current charges before you order, so you are not surprised by charges you were not expecting.

03. Terms

Terms and Conditions

Please be aware that your parcel can be also delayed by customs and subsequent charges can be applied. We have no control over this, and cannot supply any details why or for how long your parcel will be delayed.

Returns and Exchanges

In order to be eligible for a refund, items must be returned to us unused, in original packaging, and with all original brand tags still attached. Please include a note in the return stating the reason for your return, your name, address and what you wish to do – exchange (Specify size) or refund. Please include a phone number just in case we have any questions. We will contact you if there is a difference in value to the original transaction to organise payment or refund. Customers will be asked to cover the cost of dispatching exchanged items. Refunds will be credited to your original method of payment.

Returns by Post

Any refunds will be credited to your original payment method, please note that credit card companies only allow refunds to be made to the card used to pay for the original order. Please be aware that it can take as long as seven working days for a refund to be acknowledged by your credit card company
